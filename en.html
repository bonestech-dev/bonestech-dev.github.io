About Us

Bone's is an innovative company focusing on orthopedic intelligent diagnosis and treatment systems, with the ambition to help prevent and treat skeletal system diseases through innovative technologies combining digital and pharmacological devices.

Founded by professors, PhD students and alumni of the University of Hong Kong, Bone's has accumulated nearly 30 years of experience in orthopedic clinics, research and industry, and has successfully developed a quantitative computed tomography bone density diagnostic technology that combines artificial intelligence and biomechanical systems to dramatically increase the accuracy of traditional bone quality testing from 60% to 90%.

Through the accurate measurement of bone quality, Bone's digitizes the human skeleton to create a complete product matrix, including orthopedic treatment all-in-one machine, intelligent minimally invasive orthopedic robot, digital internal fixation consumables, and combined pharmacological and mechanical products.

Bone's jointly owns more than 100 U.S. and Chinese patents and software copyrights, and has received honors such as 2021 Deloitte Hong Kong Technology Fast 20 & Rising Star, Sequoia X-Plan Leadership Program, 2019 AO Trauma Science Conference Award, and International Conference on Osteoporosis and Bone Research Award.

Orthopaedic Diagnostic Pain Points

The Seventh National Census of the People's Republic of China shows that the population aged 60 and above in China is 264.02 million, accounting for 18.70% of the total population, and the number of elderly people has nearly doubled in the past two decades. With the further increase of population aging, the number of bone pain, bone degeneration and fracture cases caused by osteoporosis remains high, and it is expected that 1/3 of aging women will suffer from vertebral compression fracture, and China will continue to face huge medical pressure caused by aging for a period of time in the future, and there is an urgent need for innovative treatment equipment and medical materials to meet medical needs.

Bone's Osteoporosis Positioning Therapy Products

Be ready to handle the disease before it happens.

Bone's Pharmacological Combination Product combines robotics and minimally invasive puncture technology to treat localized bone defects in elderly patients with osteoporosis through biodegradable bone filling materials as transport carriers, combined with drugs and agents that inhibit bone resorption and promote bone regeneration. The pharmacological combination product has completed in vitro biological evaluation and animal experiments, and is in the process of registration filing with the CFDA, which is expected to be approved in 2024, bringing benefits to hundreds of millions of elderly osteoporosis patients.
